# 🤝 Contributing to the IPI Protocol

First, thank you for taking the time to contribute! By joining **Project IPI**, you are helping build a universal, open-source infrastructure designed to make the sustainable choice the most economically viable choice for everyone.

As of **March 1st, 2026**, we are in the **Bootstrap Phase**. We need expertise in three critical areas: **Computer Science**, **Environmental Science**, **Blockchain Engineering**, and **Fiscal Policy**.

---

## 1. How You Can Help

### Science & Methodology (`/Methodology`)
- **Impact Weighting:** Help us refine the aggregation of the 16 PEF categories into the single IPI score.
- **Data Quality:** Review the **DQR (Data Quality Rating)** coefficients to ensure they effectively penalize greenwashing.
- **Sector Mapping:** Contribute to specific **PEFCR** (Category Rules) mappings for new industries (e.g., Electronics, Food, Automotive).

### Technology & Engineering (`/Technical-Stack`)
- **Blockchain:** Develop Smart Contracts for the **EBSI** (European Blockchain Services Infrastructure) to handle automated IPI inheritance.
- **Interoperability:** Build JSON-LD schemas and API definitions for the **Digital Product Passport (DPP)**.
- **Integrations:** Create "VAT-IPI Bridge" prototypes for existing ERP and POS (Point of Sale) systems.

### Economics & Policy (`/Fiscal-Models`)
- **Revenue Neutrality:** Run simulations to ensure **Bonus-Malus** models remain budget-neutral for sovereign nations.
- **Social Justice:** Propose mechanisms to protect low-income households from price spikes on essentials.
- **Legal Frameworks:** Draft legislative templates for **VAT Modulation** based on the IPI "Measuring Rod."

---

## 2. Our Workflow

1.  **Find an Issue:** Browse the [Issues tab](https://github.com) for "Help Wanted" or "Good First Issue" labels.
2.  **Fork & Branch:** Fork the repository and create your branch (e.g., `feature/blockchain-logic` or `fix/co2-weighting`).
3.  **Submit a Pull Request (PR):**
    *   Clearly describe the problem you are solving or the feature you are adding.
    *   Cite your scientific or legal sources (e.g., [JRC EF 4.0](https://eplca.jrc.ec.europa.eu)).
    *   Include the statement: *"I agree to the Project IPI [CLA](CLA.md) and [LICENSE](LICENSE)."* in your PR description.

---

## 3. Guiding Principles

*   **Science First:** All methodological changes must be backed by peer-reviewed Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) data or official EU PEF guidelines.
*   **Universal & Sovereign:** The IPI is a technical tool. We respect the fiscal sovereignty of all nations. We provide the "Measuring Rod"; they choose the "Tariff."
*   **Transparency:** No "black-box" logic. Every calculation must be fully auditable by any independent body or citizen.
