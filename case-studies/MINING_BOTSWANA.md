# 💎 Case Study: Sustainable Mining in Botswana
**Scenario:** High-Efficiency Diamond Extraction vs. Traditional High-Impact Mining
**Objective:** Demonstrating IPI as a Global Competitive Advantage for Developing Nations.

---

## 1. The Context: The "Green Premium" Problem
Botswana, a global leader in diamond production, has invested heavily in solar energy and water recycling for its mining operations. However, in traditional markets, a "Clean Diamond" from Botswana often competes at the same price and tax level as diamonds extracted using coal-based energy and high-waste processes in other jurisdictions.

---

## 2. The IPI Differential: Data-Driven Performance
Under the **IPI Protocol**, the environmental footprint is **Actor-Specific**, not based on industry averages.

### Scenario A: Botswana "Solar-Minimizer" (High-Efficiency)
*   **Energy Mix:** 70% Solar / 30% Grid.
*   **Water Impact:** 90% recycling rate (Primary Data).
*   **Logistics:** Optimized rail/sea route with carbon offsets.
*   **Calculated IPI Score:** **42.54** (Well below the 100 neutral benchmark).

### Scenario B: Global "Coal-Maximized" Competitor (Standard Impact)
*   **Energy Mix:** 100% Coal-fired grid.
*   **Water Impact:** Open-loop system (secondary data used).
*   **Logistics:** High-emission air freight.
*   **Calculated IPI Score:** **152.26** (High-pollution penalty).

## 4. Protecting Global Producers: The "Anti-CBAM" Shield
The [EU Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism (CBAM)](https://taxation-customs.ec.europa.eu) is often criticized as "Green Protectionism." The IPI Protocol flips this narrative:

1.  **Direct Market Access:** By using the [EBSI Blockchain](https://ec.europa.eu) to provide immutable primary data, Botswana's producers prove their performance.
2.  **No Arbitrary Penalties:** Instead of being hit by a "Country Average" carbon tax, Botswana's high-efficiency actors are rewarded for their specific investments.
3.  **Local Economic Growth:** The lower VAT in export markets increases demand for Botswana's sustainable products, fueling local GDP and [SDG goals](https://sdgs.un.org).
