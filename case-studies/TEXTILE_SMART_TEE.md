# 👕 Case Study: The "Smart T-Shirt" Battle
**Scenario:** Ultra-Fast Fashion (High-IPI) vs. Circular Local Production (Low-IPI)
**Objective:** Demonstrating how IPI-based VAT kills Greenwashing and rewards Durability.

---

## 1. The Context: The Hidden Cost of a €10 T-Shirt
In 2024, a standard t-shirt from an ultra-fast fashion giant appeared cheaper because its environmental destruction (water pollution in Asia, microplastics, air freight) was not taxed. Under the **IPI Protocol (2026)**, these externalities are quantified and integrated into the final VAT calculation.

---

## 2. Product Comparison: IPI Scoring

### Product A: The "Linear-Global" T-Shirt (High-IPI)
*   **Material:** Virgin polyester (oil-based).
*   **Production:** High-water stress region, coal-powered factory.
*   **Logistics:** Air-freight from Asia to Europe.
*   **Data Quality:** Secondary/Generic data (No transparency).
*   **Calculated IPI Score:** **378.53** (Due to the +50% **Default Penalty** for lack of primary data).

### Product B: The "Circular-Local" T-Shirt (Low-IPI)
*   **Material:** 50% recycled cotton / 50% organic linen.
*   **Production:** Closed-loop water system, 100% renewable energy (EU-based).
*   **Logistics:** Rail and electric truck delivery.
*   **Data Quality:** Verified Primary Data on [EBSI Blockchain](https://ec.europa.eu).
*   **Calculated IPI Score:** **0.77** (High-efficiency bonus).

---

## 4. The Durability Factor (The "IPI Life-Extension")
The IPI Protocol rewards products designed to last.
1.  **Repairability:** If the [Digital Product Passport (DPP)](https://commission.europa.eu) shows a high repairability score, the IPI is further lowered.
2.  **Second Life:** When the t-shirt is resold on a circular platform, the **IPI stays with the product**. The second-hand transaction can benefit from a **0% VAT**, incentivizing the circular economy over new production.

## 6. Conclusion: Protecting the European Textile Industry
The IPI Protocol acts as a natural shield for European SMEs. By forcing ultra-fast fashion actors to either **disclose their true impact** (and pay the malus) or **improve their supply chain**, the IPI levels the playing field for local, high-quality manufacturers who already invest in sustainability.
