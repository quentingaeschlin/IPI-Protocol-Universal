# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) - Project IPI
**Version:** 1.0.0-beta  
**Context:** Global Open-Source Protocol for Environmental Fiscality

---

### 1. Does the IPI Protocol infringe on National VAT Sovereignty?
**No.** The IPI is a technical **"Measuring Rod"**, not a tax rate. It provides a standardized, scientific value for a product's impact. Sovereign nations (e.g., [EU Member States](https://taxation-customs.ec.europa.eu) or [Botswana](https://www.burs.org.bw)) retain 100% control over their tax brackets. They simply use the IPI score as a "trigger" to assign a product to an existing VAT bin.

### 2. Won't this cause inflation on essential goods?
The IPI is designed as a **Revenue-Neutral "Bonus-Malus" system**. 
- **The Malus:** High-pollution products face higher VAT rates.
- **The Bonus:** This extra revenue is used to fund **lower VAT rates** on low-impact essentials. 
The goal is to shift consumption toward efficiency without increasing the total cost of the consumer basket.

### 3. How does this protect SMEs from administrative burdens?
The IPI leverages the [Digital Product Passport (DPP)](https://commission.europa.eu) and the [EBSI Blockchain](https://ec.europa.eu) to automate data flow:
- **IPI Inheritance:** SMEs automatically "inherit" the scores of their certified raw materials.
- **Simplified Tools:** We provide open-source calculation engines (like this repository) to lower the barrier to entry for small-scale actors.

### 4. How do you prevent Greenwashing?
Unlike voluntary labels, the IPI is based on the **Product Environmental Footprint (PEF)** methodology and requires **Verified Primary Data**. If an actor provides unverified or generic data, the [DEFAULT_PENALTY.md](Methodology/DEFAULT_PENALTY.md) logic applies a significant pollution multiplier, making greenwashing fiscally expensive.

### 5. Why is this project hosted on GitHub?
Transparency is the best antidote to corruption and lobbying. By open-sourcing the **IPI Protocol**, we ensure:
- **Scientific Peer-Review:** Anyone can audit the mathematical weights.
- **Interoperability:** Developers can build "VAT-IPI Bridges" for any retail software.
- **Public Trust:** The "Measuring Rod" becomes a global public good, not a proprietary black box.

### 6. Is the IPI compatible with WTO rules?
**Yes.** Because the IPI applies the same scientific criteria to both domestic and imported goods based on **actual verified performance**, it is non-discriminatory and data-driven, aligning with the principles of the [Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism (CBAM)](https://taxation-customs.ec.europa.eu).
