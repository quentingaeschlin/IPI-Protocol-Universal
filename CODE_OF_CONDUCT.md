# Code of Conduct: Project IPI

## Our Pledge
We are committed to making participation in the IPI Protocol a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, nationality, or religion.

## Our Standards
- **Focus on Science:** Arguments regarding the IPI methodology must be based on peer-reviewed environmental science (PEF/LCA).
- **Global Respect:** We acknowledge that the IPI is universal. We respect the fiscal sovereignty of all nations, from the EU to Botswana.
- **Transparency:** We do not tolerate "Greenwashing" or the manipulation of data to favor specific industries.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project leads.
