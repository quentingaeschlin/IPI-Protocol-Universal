# 🏭 Actor-Specific Differentiation: Rewarding Real Performance
**Protocol Version:** 1.0.0-beta  
**Standard Reference:** [EU PEF Primary Data Collection Requirements](https://green-forum.ec.europa.eu)

---

## 1. The Core Principle: "Average is the Enemy"
Traditional environmental labels often rely on **Secondary Data** (industry averages). This creates a "free-rider" problem where polluting companies hide behind the good performance of their sector. 

The **IPI Protocol** mandates **Actor-Specific (Primary) Data** to ensure that every investment in sustainability results in a direct fiscal advantage via [VAT Modulation](../Fiscal-Models/VAT_MODULATION.md).

---

## 2. Primary vs. Secondary Data
The IPI Score ($S_{IPI}$) is heavily influenced by the **Data Quality Rating (DQR)**:


| Data Type | Definition | IPI Impact (DQR Factor) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Data** | Measured at the specific factory/process (e.g., actual kWh of solar used). | **1.0 (No Penalty)** |
| **Secondary Data** | Estimated based on regional or global industry averages. | **1.2 (+20% Penalty)** |
| **Missing Data** | No data provided for a mandatory PEF category. | **1.5 (Default Penalty)** |

---

## 3. The "Incentive Loop"
1. **Measurement:** An actor (e.g., a textile mill in Portugal) measures its real-time water and energy consumption.
2. **Verification:** A certification body verifies the data as part of the **CE Marking** process.
3. **Tokenization:** The verified data is anchored on the [EBSI Blockchain](../Technical-Stack/BLOCKCHAIN_EBSI.md).
4. **Differentiation:** The product receives a **Specific IPI (e.g., 65)**, while a competitor using generic data remains at **120**.
5. **Fiscal Reward:** At the retail level, the Specific IPI product is taxed less, increasing its market share.

---

## 4. Supply Chain Aggregation (The "IPI Multiplier")
Because the IPI is inherited through the supply chain, choosing "Actor-Specific" suppliers has a multiplier effect:
*   If a manufacturer chooses 5 suppliers with **Verified Primary IPIs**, the final product's uncertainty is near zero.
*   The final **Digital Product Passport (DPP)** displays a verified PEF making it more attractive to institutional buyers and on the shelf, to conscious consumers.

---

## 5. Transition for SMEs
To ensure [SME competitiveness](https://commission.europa.eu), the IPI Protocol provides:
*   **Safe Harbors:** Simplified primary data templates for small-scale actors.
*   **Collective Primary Data:** Local clusters (e.g., an organic farming cooperative in Botswana) can certify a shared "Cluster IPI" to reduce individual audit costs.
